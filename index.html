About

The Church of God of Saint Petersburg represents a local body of believers thats part of the universal church of God worldwide. Our doors are open to anyone searching for love from God and unity with believers in God. We are a humble people living a righteous life through his grace. We aim to do good works in our local community by helping, supporting, and uplifting the spirits of the less fortunate. Furthermore, we are confident that God will bring his children together and build them up in the faith and discover their true lifes purpose.
